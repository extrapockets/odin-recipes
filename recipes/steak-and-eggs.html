Steak and Eggs

Description:

This is an easy, healthy and delicious dish that is good not just at breakfast, but at any time of day.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 Tbsp Butter
  • Salt, Pepper, and any other desired seasoning

Directions:

  1. Add butter to skillet and heat on medium
  2. Season steak with salt, pepper, etc. as desired
  3. Add steak to skillet and cover for 4 minutes
  4. Turn steak over and add 2 eggs. Cover skillet for 5 minutes
  5. In 5 minutes, or when eggs are cooked to your liking, turn off heat and remove cover
  6. Let cool 1 minute, then add to a plate and enjoy!
