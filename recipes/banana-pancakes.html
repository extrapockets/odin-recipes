Banana Pancakes

Description:

These pancakes add banana for a natural sweetness

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • An Egg
  • Milk
  • Avocado Oil
  • Bananas

Directions:

  1. Combine your dry ingredients (flour, sugar, salt, baking powder) in one bowl and your wet ingredients (egg, milk, avocado oil, mashed bananas) in another bowl. Add the dry ingredients to the bowl with the wet ingredients, then stir until they're incorporated. It's OK if your batter is slightly lumpy.
  2. Pour the batter in Â¼ cup portions onto a lightly oiled pan or griddle over medium-high heat. Cook for a few minutes, flip with a spatula, and cook for another few minutes (or until each side is golden brown).
  3. Serve your banana pancakes immediately. They're delicious alone or with your favorite pancake toppings.
